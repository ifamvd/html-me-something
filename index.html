My Favorite St. Louis Eateries

There are so many different places here in St. Louis where you can grab delicious food to eat. Here are some of my favorites. And rather than believing my words, you can read below what others have said about these places. Disclaimer: They are not in any particular orders. I like them equally depending on how I feel. BBQ joints happened to be on top because I was craving BBQ when I was working on this.

Sugarfire Smokehouse

Excellent smoked meats, hard to choose just one! Their sides are fresh and great quality, with my favorite restaurant version of baked beans. They often have unique specials too (my husband tried one that had rib cakes as a bun)! They usually have dessert options by the cashier as well, courtesy of their nearby pie shop. I recommend their signature Sugarfire Pie!

I love BBQ and when I am in a place that is known for it I make it a point to seek out some smoked meat! Sugarfire is a counter style eat in or take out place. We got the baby back ribs and the brisket with a variety of sides, everything was perfectly cooked and tasty even without sauces. I am a huge brisket fan, Sugarfire's was juicy and tender, my mouth is watering just thinking about it. The staff was knowledgeable, friendly, and eager to serve.

This place was awesome! The food was perfect. The soda fountain was not the "big boys" as i think it was much better! The service was beyond helpful and did i mention the food? OMG! Will be back again for sure. More of the BBQ beans, more brisket, more scalloped potatoes. Can't go wrong here!

Pappy's Smokehouse

UNBELIEVABLE ribs! I’m a bit skeptical whenever anyone claims having the #1 anything. Well, I’ve had ribs in 3 different continents at several restaurants and have to say their ribs win #1 in my list! Service was amazingly fast. You sit and suddenly their bringing your order. Prices are greatly affordable $30 for two without drinks half slab and brisket with two sides.

If you don't go to Pappy's when visiting, you're not visiting the Lou right. If you don't eat here and you live in STL, are you even living? Pappy's is, hands down, second to none, hometown BBQ that no one could ever hate. It doesn't matter what meat you order, because it's going to taste good. The beans? Yeah, they've got pork in em. The potato salad and slaw can't be beat. Get out here and eat

You must try this. Amazing food and sauce. Very efficient almost cafeteria style ordering. Worth every penny. You probably won't be there for more than an hour including eating.

Corner 17

I always enjoy the food and especially the bubble tea here. I find myself coming back here often. Definitely recommending this place if you enjoy good Chinese food and bubble tea.

Their noodles are absolutely delicious. I got the hand pulled stir fried noodles with shrimp and they were amazing. Also, if you are looking for something sweet, I highly recommend their fluffy ice. They have in a couple of different flavors. It's so light and refreshing, especially on these hot, summer days. Their bubble is also incredibly delicious as well. The food is always good everytime I go there.

Super yummy! The veggie delight is my favorite dish and the portion was sufficient to fill me up. The food is flavorful and the ambient here is comfortable and chill. I am studying at WashU for the summer so it's also conveniently located next to the university.

Yummy Cafe

I had been craving uighur, northern chinese lamb dishes and I definitely got my fill here. Delish!!! The gentlemen who served me was fantastic. I will definitely be going back.

Soooo Yummy. We had the pork lung appetizer with white rice, the lamb chili pot, beef fried rice and the pork liver with vegetables. Everything was delicious and the portions were huge. HUGE! I brought home 2 boxes and a bucket. I made 2 meals out of it already and there is still more. The flavors and textures are amazing. Great customer service. Will recommend and will return.

Our family went to visit Washington University at St. Louis in the summer. We found this small restaurant by accident and decided to give it a try. We are from Cleveland and like to sample Chinese food in the big cities. I cook Chinese food well at home and am usually picky about the qualities of Chinese food served in Chinatowns. I have to admit Yummy 17 serves genuine Chinese food! Their dumplings are among the best I have ever had. Great taste, great price, great service! Look forward going there next time in town. One thing I don't understand is why they don't accept tips.

WuDon Korean BBQ

First time doing Korean BBQ. The staff are really great, they trade out the grills so it doesn't get so burnt. I don't suggest the all you can eat for less than 4. We only got through 3 of the meat. If you are going for two, ask for half portions. If you ever get confused ask, they are really chill staff. You could do glutton and grain free here too, it is possible.

Professional and extremely delicious entrees. Decided to try the all you can eat grill for my first time there. You get to cook your own food on a small electric grill positioned in the middle of the table. I normally go for top items first to get the best experience. Not at all disappointed with the results. Several side dishes including kimchi and plates of tender, marinated chicken and beef. Seating was comfortable and not much of a wait. For the younger crowd, if your nights just getting started, Pink Galleon is right next door.

Absolutely amazing! First timer- the staff got so busy, yet they exercised patience and kindness in explaining between bbq'ing and just regular dine in. I'm absolutely happy and will be back more often. Thank you for amazing service in the middle of chaos!

Nudo House STL

This spot is a real gem on Olive! Food is creative, fresh, and very flavorful. There are specials every day (which are sometimes really unique!) that are well worth trying. The place has an energetic vibe and the staff are extremely friendly. They do take-out as well.

I'm a huge fan of Mai Lee, so it's no surprise that I love this place which is a spin-off of Mai Lee focused on just pho and ramen. The broths are incredibly flavorful and filled with the freshest vegetables. The menu is quite small, but there are no bad options; they also have specials all the time if you want to try an often-unique creation. The service is very quick making Nudo House a perfect place to go for a lunch break. Well-deserved 5 stars!

Had the tamarind fried chicken von mi was so awesome. Everything is always so good and taste are fresh and vibrant. Keep up the Great Work!
